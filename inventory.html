Graco partially follows the lean principles. They violate the overproduction and excess inventory wastes, but for strategic reasons.

Graco keeps an average of 3 weeks of inventory. They use inventory "turns" as their primary metric for managing inventory. They use a FIFO inventory system.

Graco strategically allocates more resources to inventory because their customers expect repair and replacement parts in 1-2 days.
